Completos

A loaded hot dog topped with avocado, tomato, and mayonnaise, famously rich and bursting with flavor.

A beloved and indulgent version of the hot dog, known for its generous and flavorful toppings. It starts with a soft bun and a classic sausage, but what sets it apart are the layers of fresh ingredients piled on top. The most iconic version, the completo italiano, includes chopped tomatoes, mashed avocado, and a thick swirl of mayonnaise â€” resembling the colors of the Italian flag. The completo is a staple of Chilean street food and casual dining culture.

Ingredients

  • 4 hot dog buns
  • 4 hot dogs
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 avocados
  • vegetable oil-to taste
  • Salt-to taste

For the mayonnaise

  • 1 cup vegetable oil (200-250 ml)
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • Salt-to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the hot dogs in boiling water for 5 to 10 minutes.
  2. Toast the hot dog buns to your liking.
  3. Dice the tomatoes, add oil and salt to taste.
  4. Mash the avocado, add oil and salt to taste.
  5. For the mayonnaise, place all the ingredients in a container and try to stir as little as possible. Place the immersion blender at the bottom of the container and turn it on at maximum power. When you see the emulsion start to form, slowly lift the blender until the mayonnaise is fully formed.
  6. In a bowl, combine the egg whites with the sugar and place over a gentle double boiler, stirring occasionally until the sugar crystals are completely dissolved.
  7. Assemble the completo like a hot dog, following the order: sausage - tomato - avocado - mayonnaise and enjoy.
